About

An outline of my journey to where I am today

  • May 2017 - November 2017

    Moringa School

    I had the God given chance of being a part of Moringa School, a world-class software developer accelerator based in Nairobi, Kenya. I learnt the JavaScript frameworks of Angular and Ember, Java and the android framework and frontend libraries such as Bootstrap and JQuery.

  • September 2016 - April 2017

    Geospatial Research International

    As a GIS Assistant, I had the opportunity to practice my GIS knowledge and serve the people of Kenya. We travelled across the country to various Water Service Providers while mapping their coverage areas. Water is a right!!

  • June 2016 - September 2016

    Rwathia Distributors LTD, Kiewa Group LTD and Viatech Services LTD (Consortium) (RKV)

    As a GIS Technician, I participated in the Last Mile electrical connection project by providing useful map information that was used for the basis of design of the network distribution and estimating project costs.

  • May 2016 - December 2017

    Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology

    Took my BSC in Geomatic Engineering covering units in Land Surveying and Geospatial Information Systems.
